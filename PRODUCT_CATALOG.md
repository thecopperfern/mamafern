## Overview

The initial launch catalog consists of **10 products** designed to enable all four gift bundles and populate six collections. Every product shares the Mama Fern earthy aesthetic: sage greens, cream, warm brown, and terracotta on organic cotton blanks.

### Design Language
- **Graphics:** Small, hand-drawn-style botanical illustrations (ferns, leaves, wildflowers, acorns, mushrooms)
- **Typography on garments:** Handwritten-style script or clean sans-serif short phrases
- **Placement:** Left chest (small), center chest (medium), or large back print
- **Color palette on garments:** Single-color or two-color prints to keep screen printing costs low
- **Blank colors:** Cream/Natural, Sage Green, Oatmeal/Heather, Dusty Rose

## Design Brief Summary

All designs should feel:
- **Hand-drawn**, not digitally perfect — like a nature journal sketch
- **Single or two-color** to keep screen printing costs manageable
- **Botanical and nature-focused** — ferns, wildflowers, leaves, mushrooms, acorns
- **Warm and grounded** — organic feel, not trendy or loud
