Fabric & Materials

Organic Cotton Standard

All Mama Fern garments use GOTS-certified organic cotton — grown without synthetic pesticides, processed without harmful chemicals, and independently audited from field to finished garment. This is our non-negotiable quality standard.

Why It Matters

Our customers choose organic because what touches their family's skin matters. GOTS certification is the gold standard — it's not a marketing label. Every batch is traceable, every mill is audited, and every garment is tested for harmful residues.
