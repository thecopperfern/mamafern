Brand Story & Mission

Mama Fern is a family apparel brand rooted in simplicity, nature, and intentional living. We make clothes for the parents who choose organic cotton, who prefer a walk in the woods over a day at the mall, and who believe what touches their family's skin matters just as much as what's on the table.

Who We Are

A direct-to-consumer family clothing brand built on organic cotton and natural fabrics. We design minimal, botanical-inspired apparel for moms, dads, kids, and babies ‚Äî with matching family sets as our core differentiator.

Who We Serve

Millennial and Gen-Z "crunchy" parents ‚Äî the ones who read ingredient labels, choose wooden toys, and gravitate toward earth tones. Gift buyers (grandparents, friends) are our secondary audience, especially around Mother's Day.

Usage Rules

‚úì DO
  • Use Playfair Display 900 for all wordmark applications
  • Maintain minimum 24px clear space around the wordmark
  • Use only approved color combinations (dark-on-light or light-on-dark)
  • Minimum reproduction size: 80px wide (digital) / 20mm (print)
  • Use on cream, white, or fern-dark backgrounds
‚úó DON'T
  • Replace Playfair Display with another font
  • Add drop shadows, outlines, or effects to the type
  • Stretch, compress, or rotate the wordmark
  • Place on busy backgrounds or photographs without contrast overlay
  • Use fern on terracotta or other low-contrast pairings
Garment Text: "mama fern" on products uses a custom handwritten script style (generated via Nano Banana Pro), not the Playfair Display wordmark. The display wordmark is for web, print, packaging, and marketing materials only.

Voice & Tone

Mama Fern sounds like the friend who recommends the best farmers market ‚Äî warm, knowledgeable, never preachy. We speak to parents as equals, not consumers.

Personality Traits

Warm

Like a handwritten note, not a corporate memo. Personal, human, genuine.

Grounded

Factual about materials and craft. No greenwashing or vague "eco" claims.

Playful

Light and fun when talking about family life. We get the chaos and love it.

Calm

Never urgent, never pushy. No "LAST CHANCE!" or countdown timers.

Photography & Imagery

Our visual world lives at the intersection of nature journal and family scrapbook. Warm light, natural settings, real moments.

Photography Direction

‚úì AIM FOR
  • Golden hour natural light ‚Äî warm, soft, directional

  • Authentic family moments: holding hands, laughing, exploring
  • Cream/oat should always be the hero colorway in first photos

  • Flat-lay product shots on natural textures (linen, wood, stone)
‚úó AVOID
  • Harsh studio lighting or pure white backgrounds

  • Cluttered backgrounds or urban settings
  • Heavy filters or over-saturated editing
